Clinical Scenario


The scenario here explains about the patient who approaches hospitals to get medical care, his entire medical record (Patient Medical Data - PMD) is maintained. This includes the patients entire medical history. A new record is created for any change in his medical status


A patient medical interaction with a health care provider would contain


  • Patient Information
  • Current and historical medical information
  • Consultation reports
  • Cardio diagnosis
  • Vital signs of the patient
  • Lab tests
  • Medications
  • Surgical and operative reports
  • Doctor's notes
  • Discharge Notes


The above information are recorded and maintained by the health care provider. Patients medical records are also sent to appointed insurance companies for claim processing.
