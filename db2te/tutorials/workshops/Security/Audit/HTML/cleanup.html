Remove and drop audit policies


You must log into the database as Pat before proceeding with this page.

Remove and drop audit policies with the following commands.

  • AUDIT TABLE ... REMOVE POLICY removes the audit policy against the 'credit_card' table
  • AUDIT ROLE ... REMOVE POLICY removes the audit policy against the 'custService' role
  • AUDIT SYSADM, DBADM REMOVE POLICY removes the audit policy against the SYSADM and DBADM authorities
  • DROP AUDIT POLICY drops the audit policies
