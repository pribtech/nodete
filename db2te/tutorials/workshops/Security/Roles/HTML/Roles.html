Roles


A database role is essentially a database object that groups together one or more privileges or database authorities. Roles can be granted to users, groups, PUBLIC, trusted context, or other roles.

To be compliant with PCI requirement "Protect Stored Data", the J.K.Avro superstore decides to implement DB2 security feature: roles.

  • The security administrator (SECADM) creates two roles: custService and Mgr.
    • Employees of customer service department are granted 'custService' role.
    • Managers of customer service department are granted 'Mgr' role.
    • The SECADM grants SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE and DELETE privileges to these roles
    • The SECADM grants roles to users.
