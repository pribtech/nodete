Security Challenges


In today's business world data privacy and data security are becoming a major concern not only for individuals but also for business organizations.
  • Data privacy refers to the evolving relationship between technology and the legal right to, or public expectation of privacy in the collection and sharing of data.

  • Data security is the means of ensuring that data is kept safe from corruption and access to it is suitably controlled.


Security Standards


By the global nature of the Web, data privacy and data security compliance include both US and non-US security regulations. DB2 security features enable organizations to be compliant with standards.

One such standard is Payment Card Industry (PCI) Data Security Standard (DSS). This regulation is owned and distributed by the PCI Security Standards Council. It is a collaborative effort between major credit card companies and is designed to protect customers' personal information.
