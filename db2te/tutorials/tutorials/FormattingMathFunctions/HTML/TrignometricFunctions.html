The following trigonometric functions have been added to the SYSIBM schema: ATANH, COSH, SINH, TANH, ACOS, ASIN, ATAN, ATAN2, COS, COT, SIN, and TAN. ATANH, COSH, SINH, and TANH are new. The other functions were available under the SYSFUN schema; having them in SYSIBM offers a performance advantage.
