In the second example, which is a variation on the first, consider the idea of a school board. In the school board there are 5 schools, each having 10 classrooms with a capacity of 20 students. This school board can accommodate a maximum of 5*10*20 = 1,000 students.

In this case, we would like to work with student records based on three factors: the SCHOOL_ID, the CLASS_ID, and the STUDENT_NUM values. Each of these three columns will have unique values and will be used together to add, update, and delete student records.

Note: As with the previous example, other indices may be added separately and at some other time.
