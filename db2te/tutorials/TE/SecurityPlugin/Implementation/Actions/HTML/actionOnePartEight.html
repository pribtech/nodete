Parameter Checking

Steps:

  1. Make the changes to your document as indicated on the right
  2. Check your work (you may not want to delete the user/membership/group quite yet), then reload the panel containing the Db2auth Custom Master View or open it again from your menu item


A check is similar to a follow-on action, but the condition can only be compared against the value which has just been assigned to the parameter. Checks execute their tasks 'onMatch' or 'onNonMatch' of their respective condition.

Here, the check is checking for a error message strings or blank strings and will enter on match.
