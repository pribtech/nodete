Getting Your Menu Item to Load Your Stage

Now that you have defined the layout of your stage, you can tell your menu item to load it instead of the master view that it is loading currently.

Steps:

  1. Open your menu file (menu_30_Db2authLab.xml) with an editor
  2. Make the changes to your document as indicated on the right
  3. Go to File->'Reload Menu' within the Technology Explorer, not your browser, and check your work
