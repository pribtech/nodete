The Groups Tab

The Groups tab displays all content in the DB2AUTH.GROUPS table. The query for this tab display is displayed in the 'Ad hoc SQL' tab to the right.

The 'Groups' tab next to the 'Ad hoc SQL' tab is the actual Groups tab that the module pulls up. If the tab has not loaded properly, you have not installed the plug-in, are not connected to the proper database, or do not have the proper privileges. In that case, click here for a sample view.

There are two menu options:

  • Add Group
  • Delete Group

You will also see two action icons:

  •   to remove the group
  •   to display more authorization details about the group (such as privileges and roles) in the bottom details panel (see page 7 of this tutorial for a picture)

Steps:

  1. Click on the 'Groups' tab located to the right of the 'Ad hoc SQL' tab
  2. Try the different menus and icons
